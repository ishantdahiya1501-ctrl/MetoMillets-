Can I change my subscription anytime?
Yes! You can modify flavors, frequency, or quantity anytime from your account dashboard. Changes take effect at your next delivery.
Can I skip a delivery?
Absolutely! You can skip up to 3 months without canceling. Your subscription will resume automatically unless you pause it again.
How do I cancel?
Cancel anytime from your account settings. No cancellation fees. Your subscription ends at the end of the current billing cycle. If you've paid for future deliveries, we'll refund the unused portion.
What if I receive a damaged box?
Contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos. We'll send a replacement immediately at no charge. Subscribers receive expedited replacement shipping.
Can I gift a subscription?
Yes! Gift subscriptions are perfect for chocolate lovers. Purchase a subscription plan and we'll send a beautiful gift card to your recipient with activation instructions.
How does billing work?
Your payment method is charged on your selected schedule (monthly, quarterly, or annually). You'll receive a confirmation email before each billing date.
