About Me to Millets

From a passion for pure, ethical chocolate comes Me to Millets — crafting premium treats you can feel good about. Explore our healthy sugar free chocolate collection.

Our Mission

We believe chocolate should be pure, guilt-free, and ethically sourced. Every bar is crafted with 100% processed-free sugar, naturally sweetened with honey and dates, and enriched with nutrient-dense sorghum.

Our mission is simple: deliver the most indulgent chocolate experience while maintaining the highest standards of ethics, sustainability, and nutritional integrity.

Our Core Values

100% Pure

No artificial ingredients, no preservatives, no compromise. Just pure chocolate goodness in every bite.

Ethically Sourced

We partner with farmers who share our commitment to fair trade, environmental responsibility, and quality.

Sustainable

Every step of our process—from sourcing to packaging—prioritizes environmental sustainability and community welfare.

Health-Conscious

Nutrient-rich sorghum, natural sweeteners, and superfood ingredients make our chocolates as good for you as they taste.

Ethical Sourcing

We work exclusively with farms that meet our rigorous ethical standards. Every cacao bean, every sorghum grain, and every ingredient is sourced responsibly.

Our partnerships ensure fair wages, safe working conditions, and environmental stewardship. We believe that great chocolate starts with respect—for farmers, communities, and the planet.

  • Direct Relationships — Long-term partnerships with farming communities
  • Transparent — Full traceability from farm to your table

Sustainability Commitment

We're committed to minimizing our environmental footprint. Our packaging is 100% recyclable and made from sustainable materials. Our facilities operate on renewable energy.

From ocean-safe ingredients to carbon-neutral shipping options, sustainability isn't an afterthought—it's woven into every aspect of our business.

  • Eco-Friendly Packaging — 100% recyclable, plastic-free materials
  • Carbon Neutral — Renewable energy powered operations
  • Community Impact — 1% of profits support environmental initiatives
