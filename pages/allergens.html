üí° Recommendations

For Nut Allergies

Our dark chocolate range is nut-free but processed in a facility with nuts. Contact us for special precautions or dedicated sourcing options.

For Gluten Sensitivity

All our base chocolates are naturally gluten-free as sorghum is a naturally gluten-free grain. However, check specific product flavors for added ingredients.

For Dairy-Free Needs

Choose our dark chocolate collection which is 100% dairy-free. Our milk chocolates contain dairy. Look for "Vegan" or "Dairy-Free" labels.

For Vegan Requirements

Our dark chocolate with honey is not vegan (honey is animal product). Purely plant-based options marked as vegan are available. Contact for details.
